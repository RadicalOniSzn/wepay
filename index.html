How it works

One person rallies the room

No more overpriced MTN or Airtel data. If you're all in one place, you're ready to share Starlink.

1

Start a group

Name your hostel, block or camp, pick a plan and how many people will share. You get an invite code instantly.

2

Share your code

Drop the code in your hostel WhatsApp group. Everyone joins with it and sees their small share to pay.

3

Everyone pays, you go live

Each person pays their share into one account. Once the group is funded, WePay sets up, owns and manages the dish — and keeps it running. When the term ends, members chip in for the next subscription to stay online.
